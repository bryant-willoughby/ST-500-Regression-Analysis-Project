Austin Micromobility Trip Dataset - Variable Metadata

trip_id: Text - A unique ID for each trip (arbitrary, not from provider)
device_id: Text - Unique ID for the device used (arbitrary, not from provider)
vehicle_type: Text - Type of vehicle (bicycle or scooter)
trip_duration: Number - Duration of the trip in seconds
trip_distance: Number - Distance of the trip in meters
start_time: Floating Timestamp - Trip start time in UTC
end_time: Floating Timestamp - Trip end time in UTC
modified_date: Floating Timestamp - Record last modified date in US/Central time
month: Number - Month of the trip (1 = January, etc.)
hour: Number - Hour of the day the trip occurred
day_of_week: Number - Day of the week (Sunday = 0)
council_district_start: Text - Council district where the trip started
council_district_end: Text - Council district where the trip ended
year: Number - Year the trip started (US/Central time)
census_geoid_start: Text - GEOID of 2010 US Census Tract trip originated from
census_geoid_end: Text - GEOID of 2010 US Census Tract trip ended at
start_time_us_central: Floating Timestamp - Trip start time in US/Central time
end_time_us_central: Floating Timestamp - Trip end time in US/Central time
